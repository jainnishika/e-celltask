About E-Cell

Welcome to the entrepreneurship cell(E-Cell) of VIPS! The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) is a student-driven initiative that promotes innovation, startup culture and leadership within the campus. We aim to encourage students to think creatively and turn their ideas into real-world solutions.

Through workshops, speaker sessions, competitions and mentorship programs, we help young innovators build skills in entrepreneurship, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Our Mission

To empower students with resources, opportunities, and an active ecosystem that helps them develop innovative and entrepreneurial skills.

Our Vision

To build a strong community of creators and future founders capable of solving real-world problems with creativity and passion.
